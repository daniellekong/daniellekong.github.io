Foreign Domestic Workers in Hong Kong

Multimedia Story: Design | Photography | Research

In the summer of 2015, I received a grant to work on a self-initiated project that involved interviewing, writing, and photographing migrant workers in Hong Kong. I was interested in learning about the lives of foreign domestic workers -- their daily activities, motivations for leaving home countries, and use of urban spaces. The project in 2015 resulted in a photo article.

More recently, I wanted to create an interactive/multimedia web piece with the learnings and photos from this experience. I designed the flow of the story and brought it to life with HTML, CSS, and JS.

Crafted by Danielle Kong, 2018
